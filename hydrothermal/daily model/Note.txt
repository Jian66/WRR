S_YNHYDROINIT.lg4 is used to provide feasible solutions for hydro model.
S_YNHYDRO.lg4 is the hydro model.
S_YNTHERMAL.lg4 is the thermal model.
S_HYDROTHERMAL.lg4 is used to determine the boundaries of the fuel cost.
S_HYDROTHERMAL_Pareto is used to generate the Pareto front. 

Each Excel file stores the results for a point in the Pareto front.
